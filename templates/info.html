The C.L.E.A.R. Framework is a set of guidelines for crafting effective prompts that maximize the potential of interactions with Large Language Models and chatbots. It stands for:

  • Clarity: Use clear and concise language.
  • Length: Keep it brief and balance complexity.
  • Expectations: Set response boundaries and prepare for uncertainty
  • Active Language: Use action-oriented phrases and make it open-ended.
  • Relevance: Provide context and stay focused on the topic.
